Degree

Bachelor of Financial Engineering

Entry Requirement

i)Pass Foundation/Matriculation studies from a recognised institution with a minimum CGPA of 2.50 AND a Credit in Mathematics AND a Pass in English at SPM Level or its equivalent;
ii)Pass STPM or its equivalent with a minimum Grade C+ (GP 2.33) in 2 subjects AND a Credit in Mathematics AND a Pass in English at SPM Level or its equivalent;
iii)Pass A-Level with a minimum of Grade D in 2 subjects AND a Credit in Mathematics AND a Pass in English at SPM Level or its equivalent;
iv)Pass UEC with a minimum of Grade B in at least five (5) subjects inclusive of Mathematics and English;
v)Pass STAM with a minimum Grade Jayyid (Good) in 2 subjects AND a Credit in Mathematic AND a Pass in English at SPM Level or its equivalent;
vi)Any qualification equivalent to Diploma in Finance, Banking, Insurance or related field (Level 4, MQF) with a minimum CGPA of 2.50 out of 4.00;
vii)Pass DKM /DLKM/DVM with a minimum CGPA of 2.50. Candidates with CGPA below 2.50 MUST have at least two (2) years of work experience in the related field.*

Degree

Bachelor of Computer Science

Entry Requirement

i)Pass STPM in Science Stream or its equivalent with a minimum of Grade C (GP 2.00) inclusive of Mathematics and one Science / ICT subject;
ii)Pass A-Level with a minimum of Grade D in Mathematics and one Science / ICT subject;
iii)Pass UEC with a minimum of Grade B in at least five (5) subjects inclusive of Mathematics, English and one Science / ICT subject;
iv)Diploma in Computer Science OR Software Engineering OR Information Technology OR Information Systems OR equivalent and any Diploma in Science and Technology from recognised institution with a minimum CGPA of 2.50. Candidates with CGPA below 2.50 may be admitted subject to a rigorous internal assessment process;
v)Pass DKM /DLKM/DVM with a minimum CGPA of 2.50. Candidates with CGPA below 2.50 MUST have at least two (2) years of work experience in the related field.**
