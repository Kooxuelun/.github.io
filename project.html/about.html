About ST JOHN'S COLLEGE

ST JOHN'S COLLEGE is one of the world's top engineering science and technology universities that focuses on innovation and entrepreneurship.

It is located both in Kuala Lumpur, the capital city of Malaysia, Malacca, the beautiful city rich with heritage buildings,

and Johor Bahru,the southern city in Iskandar Malaysia, which is a vibrant economic corridor in the south of Peninsular Malaysia.
