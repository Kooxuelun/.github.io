Diploma

Diploma in Management

Entry Requirement

i)Pass SPM/O-Level or its equivalent with a minimum of Credit in at least three (3) subjects, and at least a Pass in English;
ii)Pass STPM or its equivalent with a minimum of Grade C (GP 2.00) in any subject AND a Pass in English at SPM Level or its equivalent;
iii)Pass STAM with a minimum Grade of Maqbul (Pass) AND a Pass in English at SPM Level or its equivalent;
iv)Pass UEC with a minimum of Grade B in at least three (3) subjects and at least a Pass in English;
v)Pass SKM (Level 3, MQF) in a related field;
vi)A Certificate (Level 3, MQF) in the related field with at least CGPA of 2.00.

Diploma

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

Entry Requirement

i)Pass SPM/O-Level or its equivalent, with a minimum of Grade C in at least three (3) subjects inclusive of Mathematics and one Science/Technical/Vocational subject and a Pass in English;
ii)Pass UEC with a minimum of Grade B in at least three (3) subjects inclusive of Mathematics and one Science/Technical/Vocational subject and a Pass in English;
iii)Pass STPM or its equivalent, with a Pass in Mathematics, English AND one relevant Science/Technical/Vocational subject at the SPM Level or its equivalent;
iv)Recognised Certificate in Engineering/Engineering Technology or its equivalent; *

Diploma

Diploma in Information Technology

Entry Requirement

i)Pass SPM/O-Level or its equivalent with a minimum of Grade C in at least three (3) subjects inclusive of Mathematics and a Pass in English;
ii)Pass UEC with a minimum of Grade B in at least three (3) subjects inclusive of Mathematics and a Pass in English;
iii)Pass STPM or its equivalent with a minimum of Grade C (GP 2.00) in one (1) subject AND a Credit in Mathematics at SPM Level or its equivalent;
iv)Pass STAM with a minimum grade of Maqbul (Pass) AND a Credit in Mathematics at SPM Level or its equivalent;
v)Pass in SKM Level 3 or any qualifications equivalent to Certificate (Level 3, MQF) AND a Credit in Mathematics at SPM Level or its equivalent.*
